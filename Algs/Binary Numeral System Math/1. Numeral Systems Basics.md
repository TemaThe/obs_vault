###### Positional numeral system 
In position numeral system 
number has two parts
unique digit and its position index

0,1 binary 
0-9 decimal 
0-7 octal
0-F hex 

Radix - number of unique digit incl 0
dec - 10
hex - 16
bin - 2

###### Bin->Dec
$$
1*2^3 + 0*2^2 + 1*2^1 + 1*2^0 = 8 + 2 + 1 = 11_{10} 
 $$
